# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}
**Owner:** {{DPO_NAME}}
**Review cycle:** Annual

## Purpose

To define acceptable use of company systems, data, and Service by employees,
contractors, and authorized users.

## Scope

This policy applies to all personnel with access to company systems, data,
or the Service.

## Policy

### Prohibited Activities

Users must not:
- Use systems for illegal activities
- Access data they are not authorized to access
- Share credentials with anyone
- Disable security controls (antivirus, MFA, screen lock)
- Install unauthorized software on company devices
- Use personal accounts for business communications containing PII
- Transmit PII over insecure channels (unencrypted email, public chat)
- Connect to untrusted networks without VPN
- Attempt to circumvent access controls

### Required Behaviors

Users must:
- Use strong, unique passwords (managed via password manager)
- Enable MFA on all accounts that support it
- Lock screens when away from device
- Report suspected security incidents within 24 hours
- Complete annual security training
- Promptly remove access for departed personnel

### Personal Use

Limited personal use of company devices is permitted provided it:
- Does not interfere with job duties
- Does not violate this policy
- Does not consume excessive resources

## Enforcement

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
Severe violations (data theft, unauthorized access) may be reported to authorities.

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually or after any significant security incident.

## Acknowledgment

All personnel acknowledge this policy at onboarding and annually thereafter.
Acknowledgment records are retained in `wiki/compliance/evidence/`.
